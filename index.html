Our Meals

Peperoni Punch

This pizza delivers a knockout combo of spicy, crispy pepperoni slices piled high on a bed of rich, gooey mozzarella and our zesty tomato sauce. Each bite hits you with a powerful punch of flavor, finishing with a satisfyingly crispy crust. Are you ready to take the punch?

Meaty Marvel

This pizza boasts a robust mix of succulent meats—savory pepperoni, and hearty sausage—brought to life with the burst of juicy cherry tomatoes. Fresh garlic adds a punch of boldness, while crisp celery brings a refreshing crunch to every bite. Get ready to be amazed!

Wild Forage

This pizza brings together the earthy richness of mushrooms, the briny bite of black olives, and the juicy freshness of ripe tomatoes, all perfectly balanced with a hearty mix of savory meats. This is the pizza that conquers all cravings—are you ready to take it on?
